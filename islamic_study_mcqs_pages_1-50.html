        What is the real meaning of Qiyas?

        Correct Answer: Judging
        Show explanation
        Explanation
        • Qiyas (قياس) is means Islamic jurisprudence (fiqh).
        • To derive legal rulings, primarily accepted among Sunni schools of law.
        • Components of Qiyas: To be valid, it requires four elements:
          • Asl (Original Case): The case with an existing ruling in the Quran or Sunnah (e.g., prohibition of wine)
          • Farʿ (New Case): The new case requiring a ruling (e.g., modern synthetic drugs).
          • Illah (Effective Cause): The shared reason for the ruling (e.g., intoxication).
          • Hukm (Ruling): The legal ruling (e.g., prohibition)
